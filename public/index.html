How It Works

One pipeline. Fully autonomous. Human-approved.

Ares runs your outbound pipeline from lead intake to booked meeting — autonomously, but always under your reps' control.

Qualify

Upload your target domains or connect your CRM. Ares enriches every company — size, industry, growth signals, digital footprint — then scores and ranks them against your best existing customers to find more like them.

Research

For each qualified lead, Ares autonomously gathers deep context: recent company news, hiring signals, product launches, competitive dynamics. Everything a great SDR would find — in seconds, not minutes.

Engage

Ares drafts personalized outreach using your playbook and your rep's voice. Every message lands in your reps' hands — WhatsApp, Slack, or email — for review. They approve, edit, or skip. Nothing sends without a human yes.
